Get Involved

There are many ways to be part of GBK Productions. Whether you are a student, a parent, a community member, or a local business, your involvement helps us create meaningful theatre experiences for students in Frankton, Lapel, and the surrounding area.

For Students

Interested in being part of a GBK production? Start by visiting our Upcoming Shows page to see what is coming next and how to get involved.

Students can participate on stage and can also contribute behind the scenes through stage crew, sound, and lights.

For Adults

We are always looking for parents and other adults who want to help support students and productions in a variety of ways.

  • Set building
  • Backstage help
  • Elementary chaperone support
  • Sound
  • Lights
  • Ticket taking
  • Concessions
