Summer Workshop

The Summer Intensive Theatre Workshop is a two-week intensive workshop preparing the show for production. Students will learn music, choreography, blocking, set building, and publicity. The Summer Theatre Workshop program is offered to all students grades 2nd - 12th regardless of school district.

The Workshop will run Monday through Thursday both weeks. The day will begin promptly at 9:30am and will finish at 1:00pm. There will be half an hour for lunchtime (self-provided) from 11:30-12:00.
